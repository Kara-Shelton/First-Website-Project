Welcome!

Welcome to CrossFit Weddington. Regardless of your fitness level, we have a place for you.


Our facility is superior to many other gyms in the area. More than just a gym, this community center is 5,000 square feet, loaded with top-of-the-line fitness equipment, located on over 3 acres of beautiful land, right here in Weddington, NC.

The members at CrossFit Weddington are Computer Engineers, Doctors, Lawyers, Police Officers, Hairdressers Firefighters, Teachers, stay-at-home moms, Recovering Food Addicts, Recovering Drug Addicts, active Alcoholics, and everything in between - no judgement. We come together with a common goal to live well and be fit. Come join us for an Intro Class.

What is CrossFit?

We are a community of diverse individuals with a similar goal in mind... work hard every day. We have high-level professional athletes, beginners, adults, children, adaptive division, and masters (age group). There truly is a place for everyone, and all are welcome. A typical day at a CrossFit gym would be back-to-back classes coached by some of the best coaches in the country. Physical ability is often measured by mobility, and that is a major focus at our gym. We are not as concerned with how much a person lifts, but whether or not they lift well. Health, happiness, and longevity are key goals at CrossFit Weddington. We teach you how to train your body for the long haul. Come in and be prepared to work. You may have to force yourself into these doors, but we are confident you will not regret it.

Class Structure

CrossFit Weddington hosts a wide range of classes at multiple times throughout the day. We try to offer as many classes as possible, so you are sure to find at least one slot that works with your schedule. Each week, we have classes from 0500 - 1200 in the mornings. Then we have an early afternoon break, and start back up with our two afternoon classes at 4:30, 5:30. Then, we have our specialty classes at 6:30 pm. You can find more information about the specialty classes by highlighting over the day in the widget below. We hope you will make time to come and say hello. If you have been looking for a sign to change your life, consider this to be it. We look forward to having you join our family.

Kid's Class 6:30 pm - All Skill Levels Welcome

Kids classes are designed to help your kid in every sport. Strength, balance, stability, and confidence are key components to a successful athlete. Our coaches will help your kid develop a foundation with a focus on longevity. Training is important, but training the right way is what truly matters.

Competition Training - 6:30 pm - Advanced Skill Levels Only

CrossFit HQ features all levels of competition. Everyhing from kids, teens, young adults, and age groups. If you have a comp approaching, you should attend this class. This is a more skilled class, so you are exprected to have a certain level of knowledge and skills to attend.

Row Conditioning 6:30 pm - All Skill Levels Welcome

Concept 2 Row, Ski, and Bike are foudnational components in CrossFit. These machines are easy on the joints, but offer an opportunity for a great workout. This Wendesday night row class focuses on conditioning and body weight movements. There may be a kettlebell, dumbbell, or medicine ball for added difficulty, but no barbells. All skill levels welcome.

Olympic Lifting - 6:30 pm - All Skill Levels Welcome

Olympic Lifting is a great way to build on the fundamental components of weight lifting. These barbell courses are designed to be slower paced, but technically focused. This is a great class for beginners, because it will help you learn the Snatch, Power Clean, Squat, Shoulder Press, and Deadlift. You will learn these things in regular classes, but these classes do not have a crossFit component of high-intensity conditioning.

Volleyball 6:00 pm

Fridays are for work and play. We have our normal class schedule throughout the day, but the last class is at 5:30. Bring your lawn chairs and coolers, invite family and friends. All are welcome to play on our beach volleyball court. This is a great way for new members to get to know everyone and have fun in the process. We are competitive, but it is all in good fun!

Intro Class for Beginners (no appointment necessary) - 9:00 am

Free intro class for anyone interested in joining the gym. If you would like to make an individual appointment for your intro class, click here and scroll to the "Select an Intro Class Date" calendar. The Intro fundamentals class helps beginners understand what a regular CrossFit workout will be like. The class ends with 20-30 minute workout.

Follwing the Intro class is our only scheduled class for the day at 10:00 am.

Founder | Cory Worf

My interest in functional fitness, and peak performance led me to a natural progression to CrossFit in which I was certified in 2008. You can find me often on our gym floor training everyone from our beginning athletes to professional athletes, and everyone in between. When I am not coaching, my favorite thing is spending time with my wife and three children. They are the most important things in my life.
