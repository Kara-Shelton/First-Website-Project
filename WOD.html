Intro Class Information

Each week, Monday - Saturday we have Introduction Classes for beginners at 0900. We require all new members attend this class, as it is an opportunity for you to meet our coaching staff and learn more about our gym. Since CrossFit is a high-intensity style of workout program, we always keep safety first. During the intro class, we will assess your mobility, strength, stamina, and so on. It will not be too invaise, but very casual.- we do not want to scare you away. We keep it light in the intro class, but we guarantee a solid workout and you will be feeling muscles you have not felt in years. If you are interested, take this opportunity to use the Calendar feature below to select your desired intro date. We are closed on Sunday, so don't pick a Sunday. Thank you for showing interest in our gym. We have a saying around here, "It is the start that stops most people." Make sure not to let the start stop you. Register for an intro class today.
